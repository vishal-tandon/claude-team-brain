# Signals: Friction Journal

Append-only. No structure required. Write what you noticed, when you noticed it.

The goal is to accumulate honest signal about what is slow, confusing, or
broken in the brain's operation. These entries feed the review ritual and
eventually drive architectural decisions.

Format: any prose is fine. Date is useful. Name is optional.

`merge=union` in `.gitattributes` makes concurrent appends conflict-free.
